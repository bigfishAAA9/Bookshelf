## English

### 📖 About This Project

This repository contains a curated collection of technical books and learning materials, currently featuring **"An Introduction to Statistical Genetic Data Analysis"** by Melinda C. Mills, Nicola Barban, and Felix C. Tropf (MIT Press, 2020).

### 🎯 Book Overview

This textbook introduces students and researchers to the emerging concepts, data, and methods of statistical genetic data analysis in an accessible, practical, and engaging manner. It is written for those who do not have a strong background in molecular biology, human genetics, or cell biology but want to integrate genetic data into their research.

### 🎓 Learning Objectives

After studying this book, readers will be able to:

- ✅ Understand fundamental terminology in human genomics
- ✅ Apply statistical methods for genetic data analysis
- ✅ Conduct genome-wide association studies (GWAS)
- ✅ Create and validate polygenic scores
- ✅ Understand gene-environment interactions
- ✅ Perform Mendelian Randomization analysis
- ✅ Address ethical considerations in genomics research

## 中文

### 📖 关于项目

本仓库包含精选的技术书籍和学习资料，目前收录了 **《统计遗传数据分析导论》**（An Introduction to Statistical Genetic Data Analysis），作者为 Melinda C. Mills、Nicola Barban 和 Felix C. Tropf（MIT Press，2020年出版）。

### 🎯 书籍概述

本书以通俗易懂、实用且引人入胜的方式，向学生和研究人员介绍统计遗传数据分析的新概念、数据和方法。本书专为那些没有深厚分子生物学、人类遗传学或细胞生物学背景，但希望将遗传数据整合到研究中的读者而写。
